## Destabilizating the Humanities
- Years of handwringing
  - systematic undervaluing of skills
  - data of current reading level and compreshension
- Broken promise of entry level, high paying jobs in STEM 
  - data on job listings 
  
## Revaluing the Humanities
- More than ever reading and writing are critical skills
  - Now = AI produced text feels generic and sub-par
  - Soon = AI produced text will feel less mediocre,
    - not because AI has gotten better
    - but because we can no longer remember what "good writing" is

## Where do we go from here?
- Stand behind the strengths of the Humanities
  - AI is not inevitable in all spheres
  - Reading and writing is not a waste of time to be skipped with AI summaries
    - ***key point*** how to thread this needle of capitalist discourse of time saved is money saved
    - wrestling with text and hard things is a valuabe skill
